To compile the beta client from source:
1. Clone the repository: \git clone https://github.com/overyr8-sys/Admin-Abuse-Client.git\
2. Run the build command: \./gradlew build\
3. The finished \.jar\ file will be located in \uild/libs/\.

## Features & Modules

### Combat
* **Auto Crystal:** Fast crystal placement and breaking.
* **KillAura:** Automatically attacks entities in range.
* **Auto Totem:** Automatically moves totems to your offhand.
* **Auto Armor:** Automatically equips the best armor in your inventory.
* **Surround:** Blocks your feet with obsidian to prevent crystal damage.
* **Criticals:** Ensures every hit is a critical hit.

### Movement
* **ElytraFly:** Enhanced flight and speed control while using Elytra.
* **Flight / Strafe:** Creative-style flight and air movement control.
* **Step / Reverse Step:** Instantly step up blocks or pull yourself down ledges.
* **No Fall:** Prevents fall damage.
* **Velocity:** Reduces or removes knockback from hits/explosions.

### Visuals & HUD
* **ArrayList:** Displays enabled modules on your screen.
* **Notifications:** On-screen alerts for toggles and events.
* **HUD Editor:** Custom placement for all on-screen elements.
* **ESP / FullBright:** See entities through walls and remove darkness.
* **Nametags:** Custom rendering for players and items.
* **New Chunks:** Highlights recently generated chunks for base hunting.
* **Watermark / Coordinates:** Displays client branding and location.

### Utility
* **Key Pearl:** Throws an ender pearl with a single keybind.
* **MCF (Middle Click Friend):** Quickly add allies to your friend list.
* **Inv HUD:** See your inventory contents without opening the menu.
* **Fast Place:** Removes the delay when placing blocks or using items.
* **Storage ESP:** Highlights chests, shulkers, and other containers.
